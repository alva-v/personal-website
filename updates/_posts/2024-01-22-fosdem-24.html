--- title: I'll be attending FOSDEM 2024 ---

FOSDEM is a european FOSS event happening every year in Brussels, this year edition being on the weekend of the 3rd and 4th of February. I'll be doing some volunteer work there.

The event is kinda corporate and sponsored by Google, Cisco, Huawei etc... If you're looking for an anticapitalist alternative, know that OFFDEM is happening at the same time. I plan on spending at least half a day there.
